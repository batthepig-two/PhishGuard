What we check
🎭
Brand impersonation
Catches sites pretending to be PayPal, Amazon, Apple, etc. on a different domain.
🔢
IP address URLs
Legitimate sites don't use raw IP addresses like 192.168.1.1 as their URL.
🌐
Risky domains
Flags high-abuse TLDs like .tk, .ml, .xyz that scammers favour.
🔐
Missing HTTPS
Login pages without HTTPS are a major red flag.
🔤
Suspicious keywords
Detects "verify", "secure", "account" combos common in phishing URLs.
➖
Hyphen abuse
Domains like my-secure-bank-login.com use hyphens to look real.
